Sanket Rane

Research Scientist at Columbia University
Eclectic Biologist Moonlighting as a Mathematician

I am a Theoretical Immunologist who ardently and sometimes a bit ambitiously tries to explain everything and anything in biology in the light of evolution. Freshly joined the Irving Institute of Cancer Dynamics, with a strong desire to build transdisciplinary collaborative projects that address fundamental issues at the forefront of Computational Immunology. I intend to develop novel integrative approaches to explain diverse immunological phenomena observed in cancer and chronic infections by combining data-driven Mathematical Models with robust Statistical methods.
